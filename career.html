Work across multiple control families including access control, identification and authentication, audit and accountability, and incident response, ensuring each control is mapped correctly to real operational practices.

Assess technical environments in Microsoft GCC High and Azure Government ecosystems, reviewing configurations in Entra ID, Intune, Defender, and related services to ensure responsibilities are properly handled under shared responsibility models. Identify gaps such as insufficient privileged user training, weak enforcement of access controls, and lack of protection for removable media handling CUI.

Document findings in formal assessment artifacts and briefing templates, supporting lead assessors in developing final outputs and client communications. Work directly with client stakeholders and internal teams to clarify control implementation, validate evidence, and ensure accurate representation of the security posture. This role builds a deep understanding of how security controls translate into real system behavior, forming the foundation for advancing into detection engineering and AI security-focused work.
