2023–2024 — Tier 3 Technical Support Engineer

Progressed into Tier 3 where I handled advanced escalations and worked beyond standard troubleshooting into system-level support and automation. Supported Zebra mobile devices used by delivery drivers for package scanning and route completion, ensuring devices were operational in real time. Used Microsoft Intune as an MDM solution to manage and control devices, including pushing firmware updates, software updates, and enforcing configuration changes remotely. Configured and maintained Intune policies to ensure devices remained compliant and functional across deployments. Performed remote troubleshooting by accessing devices directly to resolve issues affecting driver operations. Used SQL to query system data and identify patterns in device failures, and applied Python and Linux-based scripting to automate repetitive tasks and improve operational efficiency.

2022 — Tier 2 Technical Support Specialist

Started at Tier 2, handling escalations from Tier 1 related to device connectivity, mobile network issues, and operational failures in the field. Diagnosed issues preventing drivers from completing end-of-day processes, including SIM card failures, inactive or misconfigured network connections, incorrect mobile settings, and outdated Android versions. Used carrier portals such as Verizon and AT&T to validate SIM status, check IMEI details, and confirm network provisioning. Troubleshot both hardware and software issues, including device malfunctions, screen damage, and service-related replacements. Acted as a key escalation point, resolving complex issues that impacted delivery operations and ensuring minimal downtime for drivers.
